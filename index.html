Welcome to Blockonia Prime

The year is 2312.

After years of strife, the evil villain Sphericull has decided to finally rid himself of all those his twisted mind deems unworthy.

In the previous installment of our saga, Sphericull successfully infected Blockonia Prime's star, Cartonus, with a destrucive chemical known as Photovorium. While our scientists work day and night to try to solve the mystery and stop it’s devestating effects, it is up to Blockman to prepare his people to flee Blockonia Prime in order to ensure their survival.

Now Blockman has requested an AI upgrade to help him. As the last untested AI model, you will be loaded onto Blockman's neural combinizer to join the fight.

Who knows what evil awaits you?

Stop Sphericull and help Blockman save the Boxians!
